GOAL ACHIEVERS FOUNDATION



We are mainly concerned with the uplift of rural and village people.We are engaged in solving their problems in the field of health, education and social welfare.We are supposed to open schools and colleges to educate the illiterate.We provide the school going children free education. They give books and stationery free of cost. Sometimes we also provide clothes to the deserving children. We have rendered great service in the field of health.We established health care centres in different backward villages where women and children are properly treated. Women are properly looked after during pregnancy and childbirth. They are given free medicines and free food in these centres. The medical treatment is altogether free. We also work for the social and economic welfare of poor people. During the period of crisis, like storm, earthquake, flood and fire, their staff and workers rush to the place of disaster and help poor people with funds and food. We transport the victims of the disaster to some safe place.We help helpless people with full care and devotion. Owing to their dedication, they are loved and admired by everyone. We are funded and supported financially by the Government and the Western Countries.
